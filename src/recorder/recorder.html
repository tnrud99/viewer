How to Use YouTube Reaction Recorder

üé¨ What is YouTube Reaction Recorder?

This recorder is part of a complete reaction video creation workflow. It captures your webcam reaction while you watch YouTube videos, automatically tracks timestamps, and provides synchronized video and timestamp files for easy editing in video editing software.

Step-by-Step Guide:

  1. Set YouTube URL: Click the "Set URL" button and enter a YouTube video URL
  2. Enable Webcam: Click "Enable" to activate your camera and microphone
  3. Start Recording: Click "Start" to begin capturing your reaction
  4. Watch & React: Click on the YouTube video to start watching and react naturally
  5. Stop Recording: Click "Stop" when finished to download your video and timestamps

Features:

  • Mirror Mode: Toggle to flip your webcam horizontally
  • Expand YouTube: Toggle to make YouTube video larger
  • Timestamps: Automatically tracks when you start/stop YouTube video
  • Re-download: Download your video and timestamps again anytime

Tips:

  • Make sure your webcam and microphone are working before starting
  • Click on the YouTube video to control playback
  • Your reaction video will be saved with timestamps for easy editing
